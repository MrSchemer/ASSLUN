About Us

About Us

Welcome to the Active Social Service and Literary Unit Nepal (ASSLUN). We are a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering literary growth and community service in Nepal. Our mission is to provide a platform where voices can be heard, stories can be shared, and creativity can flourish. Through our initiatives, both online and in the community, we aim to create a lasting impact that enriches lives and empowers individuals.

Mission

To nurture and promote the rich literary culture of Nepal while driving positive change in society through community service and digital innovation.

Vision

A vibrant literary community where every individual has the opportunity to share their voice and contribute to the cultural and social development of Nepal.

Objectives

  • To provide a digital platform for writers, poets, and creators to publish their work and reach a wider audience.
  • To organize literary events, workshops, and competitions that encourage creativity and cultural exchange.
  • To engage in community service initiatives that address social issues and contribute to the well-being of Nepalese society.
  • To collaborate with educational institutions and literary organizations to promote literature and literacy in underserved areas.
  • To empower young and emerging writers by offering mentorship, resources, and opportunities for growth.
